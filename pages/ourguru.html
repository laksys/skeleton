OUR GURU

SWAMI JAGADATMANANDA SARASWATI is a renowned Guru, Hindu scholar, theologist, author, humanitarian, philosopher, a Mentor, Friend and Teacher in India. His devotees address him as Swamiji with reverence. He has been imparting knowledge to seekers of all age groups and backgrounds in various disciplines.

Sri Lalitambika Temple & Sri Vidya

Swamiji is an ardent devotee of Goddess Lalitambika and a Sri Vidya Upasaka belonging to the Samayachara tradition. He comes from an authentic lineage of Gurus. He has been teaching Samayachara Sri Vidya meditation, upasana and Sri Chakra worship to aspirants regardless of caste or creed. Swamiji, through the fruit of his penance has built a temple dedicated to Goddess Lalitambika at the foothills of the historical Anuvavi in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India. Sri Lalitambika Temple is one of its kind as it is the only temple in the world with Goddess Lalitambika as the chief deity. A panchaloha Mahameru is installed at the feet of Goddess Lalitambika and she is served by Goddess Lakshmi and Goddess Saraswati on either sides. The sub-deities include Lord Ganesha, Lord Karthikeya, Lord Kameshwara in the form of a Shivalinga, Lord Lakshmi Narasimha, Goddess Vishnudurga, Lord Hanuman, Lord Ananda Bhairava, Lord Danwantri, Guru Medha Dakshinamurthy, Navagrahas, Shirdi Sai Baba and Serpent Gods.

At Sri Lalitambika Temple, the Goddess is celebrated in her ten cosmic forms know as Dasa Mahavidyas. Each avatar of the Goddess represents a specific form of knowledge. The celebrations are held with pomp and splendor twice annually. Special Vedic rituals and Pujas are performed to please each Mahavidya of the cosmic Mother.

Swamiji is the founder and a trustee of Sri Lalithambiga Trust which manages Sri Lalitambika Temple. The trust provides daily meals for the underprivileged, educational and medical aids, conducts regular medical camps for eye, diabetes, women's health and geriatric. It covers the cost for medical expenses of cancer patients and gives them counseling. The trust holds yoga and meditation workshops for the youth, value education classes and leadership training programs. The Lalithambiga Trust also runs a Goshala - a shelter for cows - providing lifelong food, care and medical assistance.

Dayalaya & Anuvavi Anjaneya

>Dayalaya is an organization founded by Swamiji in memory of his Guru Pujyasri Swami Dayananda Saraswati. It manages the Dayalaya Institute for learning Advaita Vedanta, Sanskrit and Tamil and Sri Anuvavi Anjeneyar Nandavanam located at the serene and historical Anuvavi as mentioned in the Hindu Epic Ramayana.

An idol of Pujyasri Swami Dayananda Saraswati is installed at the entrance of Dayalaya Institute. It is home to several Vedic scholars, researchers and renunciates. Swamiji conducts several researches on sound therapy and healing mantras in the recording studio at Dayalaya.

https://dayalaya.org/

Sri Anuvavi Anjeneyar Nandavanam houses a 45ft high Bhakta Anjaneya. It also houses a separate shrine for Lord Rama, Goddess Sita, Lord Lakshmana and Lord Hanuman.

Within the premises of Sri Anuvavi Anjeneyar Nandavanam, a centre is dedicated for chanting of the holy mantra 'Sri Rama Jayam'. This mantra generates an extensive amount of positive energy. Devotees can record their chanting at the tally machines and the total number of chants is being kept in record. This is an initiate by Swamiji for the benefit of mankind and Mother Earth where everyone can offer their contribution.

Scholar & Teacher of Advaita Vedanta, Sanskrit and Tamil

Swamiji is an Acharya belonging to the Arsha Vidya Gurukulam Sampradaya established by Pujyasri Swami Dayananda Saraswati having obtained Sanyasa diksha from his Guru lineage.

Swamiji is well-versed in the Vedas, Upanishads, Puranas, Tantras and Agamas. He is a linguistic scholar and teacher of Sanskrit and Tamil languages. Swamiji conducts regular courses on Advaita Vedanta philosophy based on authentic Hindu scriptures and Sanskrit classes for adults as well as the youth. He has designed and taught several programs on Value Education.

Swamiji is the advisor and trustee of Arsha Vidya Gurukulam and Sruti Seva Trust, Anaikatti, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India.

Swamiji is the Honorary Dean for the Departments of Vedic Astrology and Vedanta at S-VYASA University, Bangalore.

Astrologer

Swamiji has an extensive knowledge on Vedic Astrology and Prashna Shastra. With his mastery over ancient Hindu scriptures and over three and a half decades of experience, he has helped thousands of individuals, families and businesses progress. His expertise in Vastu Shastra (traditional Hindu system of construction) has allowed him to contribute towards building of numerous Hindu temples and commercial spaces. As per his advice, several families have successfully constructed houses.

Swamiji's guidance has enabled individuals to choose the apt educational and career paths. He has contributed to the development of numerous businesses which profited and expanded. He has saved several marriages, relationships and families. Individuals suffering from illnesses for a long time were able to acquire a solution. Swamiji has counselled many with physiological issues and rectified them through Vedic Astrology.

Swamiji has authored books on Vedic Astrology, Prashna Shastra and Vastu Shastra. He has contributed significantly to the development of Astrology softwares. Currently, Swamiji is developing an application which uses the principles of Vedic Astrology to determine illnesses. Research is underway in association with doctors and software developers.

Author

Swamiji has written numerous books covering various topics, disciplines and philosophy. His books are based on holy scriptures and comprehensive research.

Swamiji has translated the holy Lalita Sahasranama, the thousand names of Goddess Lalitambika, from Sanskrit to Tamil.

He has written commentary for the sacred hymn Abhirami Anthathi comprising of 100 poems written by Sri Vidya Scholar and Upasaka Abhirama Bhattar belonging to the 18th century.

Swamiji has written an explanatory text for selected verses of the Bhagavat Gita in Tamil.

Swamiji has authored books on Samayachara Sri Vidya Meditation, Devi Upasana, Yoga, Worship of Sri Yantra, Vedic Astrology, Horary Astrology, Vastu Shastra and Value Education.

Academician

Swamiji is a diligent academician with M.A. (Philosophy and Religion), M.Phil (Philosophy) and Ph. D (Philosophy). He has presented research papers on Spirituality, Religion and Philosophy both in India and internationally.
